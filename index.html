Verify that you are a human

  1. Select a Letter: At the top of the page, you'll see individual letters. Hover over the letter you wish to use.
  2. Drag the Letter: Click and hold on the letter to grab it.
  3. Move to a Box: Drag the letter down to one of the moving boxes below.
  4. Drop the Letter: Release the mouse button to place the letter inside the box.
  5. Form Your Word: Continue dragging and dropping letters into the boxes to form a word. The order of the boxes, from left to right, will determine the order of the letters in your word.
  6. View the Result: Beneath the moving boxes, you'll see the "Resulting Word" section. This displays the word you've created based on the letters' placement in the boxes.
  7. Rearrange if Desired: Want to change your word? Simply rearrange the letters in the boxes. The resulting word will update automatically.
  8. Verify: Once you've formed the correct word, a 'Continue' button will appear. If not, you'll see a 'Try Again' button.
